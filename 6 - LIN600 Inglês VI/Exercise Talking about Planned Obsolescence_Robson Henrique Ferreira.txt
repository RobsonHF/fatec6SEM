INGLES VI

	Since companies found that creating products with a shorter duration than those created so far would guarantee greater profits, programmed obsolescence has created a world in which people buy products faster than before.
	Today people think it's cooler to have the latest cell phone, making people who don't have it the most excluded from society.
	However, this way of life is more harmful to the environment, more polluting and more selfish than living with a view to the durability of products and natural products that are less harmful to the environment.
